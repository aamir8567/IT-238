Why It's So Important

To Understand the importance of voting we first have to know why we vote. We vote to select a specific person or team to give them the ability to run our country. By voting we give them the approval to lead the way. If we dont vote we are giving up our voice and maybe that person is not the right person for the job.

Not only selecting the right person for the job is important, by not voting we are throwing out our money that we pay in tax because the wrong person will not spend the money intead that person will put it in their pockets. Another reason why voting is important is because it is our chance to demand a change.
